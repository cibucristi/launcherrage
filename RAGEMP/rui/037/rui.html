RAGE Multiplayer is an initiative started by an experienced team to provide an alternative, enjoyable and stable multiplayer experience for Grand Theft Auto V. Started in May 2016, RAGE continues to be a very delicate project with great potential seen and noticed by many. Our sole focus is to mold this project in a self-sustaining community that promotes creativity and originality. Thanks to the C++ foundation which RAGE is built upon, it does not depend on any external files or programs. RAGE is a complete, stand-alone client with great functionality. The team constantly focuses on improving performance and stability while simultaneously adding new features and expanding the API. As the future progresses, so does RAGE - and it is within both our hearts and minds to keep delivering you the best experience possible in the online GTA V community.

As of July 2017, we have announced or co-operation and merge with GTA Network. We are combining both projects and team into one to unify and strengthen the development of an alternative multiplayer experience for Grand Theft Auto V. Is it unknown what name the new project will continue under, however we are in the process of preparing and finalizing.
